私について | About Me

こんにちは! I’m Heemansh, an indie developer who loves exploring the unseen layers of technology. My passion lives in low-level systems — where bytes, memory, and assembly dance together — and in the world of cybersecurity, where every exploit is a puzzle waiting to be solved.

I create projects that blur the line between art and engineering, from hand-crafted C++ simulations to full-stack experiments. My philosophy is simple: learn deeply, build beautifully, and hack responsibly.

🐉 Inspired by Japanese indie web design, my work is about minimalism, exploration, and the joy of making things from scratch.
